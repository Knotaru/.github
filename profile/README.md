Knotaru
=======
Welcome to Knotaru. We build thoughtful, reliable software products that help teams and communities collaborate, create, and grow.

About Us
--------
Knotaru is a small, focused organization dedicated to building simple, user-centered products. Our approach emphasizes clear communication, responsible engineering, and strong relationships with users and partners.

Mission
-------
To empower teams and communities with elegant, easy-to-use technology that amplifies human collaboration and creativity.

Vision
------
A world where software removes friction from teamwork, so people can focus on meaningful work and positive outcomes.

Core values
-----------
- People first — design and decisions that respect users and teammates.
- Clarity — communicate plainly, act transparently.
- Craftsmanship — deliver high-quality work and continually improve.
- Responsibility — protect user data and behave ethically.
- Collaboration — value diverse perspectives and seek constructive feedback.

What we build
-------------
Knotaru produces a small family of products and services that work together to solve specific problems for teams. Each product has a technical implementation, but our focus is on the outcomes they enable:

- Knotaru API — A backend service that powers integrations and data flow for Knotaru products.
- Knotaru Frontend — A web experience designed for real users to interact with Knotaru's features and workflows.

If you'd like more details about product features, demo access, or a non-technical product walkthrough, contact the team (see below).

How we work
-----------
- Team structure: small cross-functional squads that include product, design, and engineering.
- Communication: public discussions live in our issue tracker and shared channels; important decisions are documented.
- Releases: frequent, incremental updates with a focus on stability and user feedback.
- Feedback: customer and partner feedback directly informs priorities.

Getting in touch

Reporting issues & suggesting features
-------------------------------------
We welcome bug reports, feature suggestions, and feedback. To get started:
1. Open an issue in the repository describing the problem or idea.
2. Include your contact preference if you want follow-up.
